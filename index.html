The Tale

Players first. Merchants second.

Lore & Order is a roving market stall run by tabletop folk for tabletop folk. We pack a wagon's worth of polyhedral treasure and roll into cons, festivals, and ren-fairs to outfit your next quest.

We're avid players ourselves — every die, tray, and one-shot on the table is something we'd happily bring to our own home game. If it isn't good enough for our party, it doesn't make it to the booth.

Whether you're hunting for the perfect set of dice, a leather dice tray that'll last a lifetime, or a one-shot to run at your next game night, you'll find your fit at our table.

The Wares

What you'll find at the table

Dice & Sets

Resin, metal, sharp-edge, glow-in-the-dark, gemstone — full polyhedral sets and one-off d20s for collectors and first-time players alike.

Accessories

Leather dice trays, hand-stitched bags, character sheet folios, GM screens, miniatures, pins, patches — the small things that make the table sing.

One-Shots

Self-contained adventures to take home and run at your next game night — pre-built scenarios with maps, NPCs, and pre-rolled characters in the box. New to GMing? We'll walk you through how to get started.
